Womankind Worldwide welcomes Olga
Ghazaryan as new Co-Chair of its Board

Posted on 20 July 2023 by Womankind Worldwide

Womankind Worldwide is pleased to announce Olga Ghazaryan as a new Co-Chair of our board. Olga who officially takes up the position on 1 August 2023, is a feminist and social justice activist with 30 years of experience in international development across 50 countries in the world. She’s worked extensively across women’s rights particularly in advancing women’s participation in leadership, ending violence against women and economic justice. She’s also previously held executive and non-executive director roles with Oxfam, Norwegian Red Cross and Water Aid- and similar capacities within the private sector.

What drew you to feminism and women’s rights work?

Growing up in Armenia I witnessed the social unrest and economic collapse in the country while in my twenties. The state of deprivation of social services and impoverishment shaped my worldview, fueled my convictions in making a difference and made me join the social justice movement.
